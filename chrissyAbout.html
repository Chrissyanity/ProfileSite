Brief Bio

After working in the auto industry for five years, I decided it was right time for a career change. I began dabbling in web development when a friend and I started our concierge service in 2014 and needed a website for people to find out about our services and book them online. I found the challenge exciting and soon after found out about the diversity defeceit in Detroit's growing tech industry.

With a little research, I found Grand Circus and saw it as the perfect gateway to a new career in tech. I enrolled in their Front-end Bootcamp and learned languages such as: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and many more that have prepared me for life as Front-end developer.

Not only am I excited to kickstart my new front-end career, I am also excited for the opportunity of sharing my knowledge with other women and girls. "Each one, teach one".
